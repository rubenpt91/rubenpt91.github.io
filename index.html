About Me 👨🏻‍💻

Nice to meet you 👋! I'm Andrea, a ML practitioner and enthusiastic traveler.

"[...] And if you find her poor, Ithaka won’t have fooled you. Wise as you will have become, so full of experience, you’ll have understood by then what these Ithakas mean." - Ithaka, C. P. Cavafy

I'm a passionate person who loves to study and discorver: it is impressive the amount of things we don't know, isn't it? I've started a PhD about Graph Neural Networks and Document Understanding in 2020, based in Universtà degli Studi di Firenze, Florence. I'm actually working as a Visiting Researcher at the Computer Vision Center (CVC) in Barcelona, for a joint program between our labs. I love travelling, meeting people and discover different cultures. When I'm not working, I really enjoy reading, playing or watching films.

For all the details about my career, you can download my Curriculum Vitae.
